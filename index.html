About this website:
This website is part of my personal portfolio as a web developer. In order to create it, I used different technologies, such as HTML, CSS and Bootstrap. The meaning of this website is to find a place for myself, in which I can gather and conserve my favourite pictures from my different trips around the world; it represents a simple way of putting togheter three things that I am passionate about: technology, travelling and photography.

Side note: all pictures you'll see in this website (backgrounds as well) were snapped by me, with only few exceptiones: underwater pictures from Indonesia have been provided by my buddy diver and dear friend Brandon Rigter, while some pictures about Sweden were taken by Edoardo Miola, the person who drove me around the Swedish Lapland and, more importantly, initiated me into photography for the first time.

Thank you, Fabio.
